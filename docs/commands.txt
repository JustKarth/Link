/mode <mode_name>
    Here mode name can be out of
    -> chat
    -> ft or fileTransfer
    -> rs or remoteShell
    -> config

transferfrom <device_to_receive_from> <remote_path_address> <local_destination_address>

union <local_directory> <remote_directory>

intersect <local_directory> <remote_directory>
